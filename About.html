Magadh Mahila College Patna University,Patna

Magadh Mahila College,established in 1946,is a women's colllege in Patna Bihar.
The College is centrally located in the heart of the capital city of Patna,Bihar.
3rd Cycle NAAC Accredited, CPE awarded and an ISO Certified Magadh Mahila College (MMC), Patna University, Patna is situated to the north of the historic Gandhi Maidan of Patna, (Bihar).
The College is situated besides the holy river Ganga which bestows a pious and peaceful environment to the campus.
The campus is spread in a 9-acre area of land with a variety of fruit-bearing trees and also large shade giving trees.
The flora and fauna conserved on the campus are the specificity of the institution.
The eco-friendly approach of the teachers, staff, students, stakeholders and vendors adds a gracious aura to the campus.
It is affiliated to Patna University,and offers undergraduate(UG) and postgraduate(PG) courses in Science,Arts and Commerce.
The College has been honoured with Best College Award on Shiskha Diwas 2014 by Sri Nitish Kumar, the chief minister of Bihar
and has also been awarded Excellent Institution of Higher Education for Girls in Bihar by His Excellency Sri Ram Nath Kovind, the Governor-cum Chancellor of Universities of Bihar,
on the occasion of National Education Bihar Summit and Award 2016.
Current Principal of Magadh Mahila is Prof.(Dr.) Namita Kumari.
